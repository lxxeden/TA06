Protection tips

These tips work together with the live tool and help users stay safer when UV exposure is higher.

Use sunscreen

Apply sunscreen before going outside and reapply when needed, especially after sweating or swimming.

Wear protective items

Hats, protective clothing, and sunglasses help reduce direct UV exposure during stronger sunlight hours.

Check conditions first

Use real-time weather and UV information before outdoor activities so you can plan safely and comfortably.

Helpful for different groups

This project can support students, workers, families, and anyone planning time outdoors.

Students

Check weather and UV before walking to class, waiting at public transport stops, or joining outdoor activities.

Workers

Useful for outdoor jobs, work breaks, and commuting during hotter and sunnier parts of the day.

Families

Great for planning school pickup, playground visits, and weekend trips with clearer weather awareness.

Sport groups

Helps teams and individuals plan hydration, sun protection, and better timing for training sessions.
